Plasma Donor!

We understand how difficult Covid-19 had been, not only for general population but equally hard for caring healthcare professionals. We wish our patients and clinicians good health and encourage you to be a plasma donor and a source of support to others when you have recovered. We also encourage you to enroll in research studies to further much needed knowledge of this new disease. Our system is designed to respect your privacy and we have safeguards to protect clinical & research information.
